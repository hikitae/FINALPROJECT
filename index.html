ABOUT

LookVR is a a complete 2D & 3D 360 capture/stitching/streaming software using state-of-the-art computational vision algorithms and GPU processing to deliver high frame rate and high resolution panoramic video. LookVR also features a modern UI designed with ease of use and on-set preview in mind which can deliver content to various platforms like Oculus CV-1, HTC-Vive, YouTube 360, Facebook 360 and other CDNs. It also can stream and process multi channel audio for use for 3D spatial sound.
Various camera rigs can be configured and calibrated on the fly in addition to pre-built rig templates for popular 360 rigs in various configurations like Blackmagic, Nokia-OZO, Freedom-360 rigs.
